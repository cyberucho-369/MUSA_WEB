Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What does score and music preparation involve?

+

Our music preparation team handles score proofreading, part extraction, transpositions, page turns, and printing. If you deliver a full score or MIDI mockup, we produce session-ready orchestral parts that arrive at the stands before downbeat.

Can you arrange accommodation for a larger production team?

+

Yes. Whether it's a single composer or a full production team of ten, we source and book accommodation near the studio to suit your group size, schedule, and preferences. We work with trusted hotels and apartments across Prague.

How far in advance should logistics be confirmed?

+

We recommend confirming logistics at least 2–4 weeks before your session date. This gives our team time to secure the best accommodation, coordinate transport, and complete all score preparation. Tighter timelines are possible — just get in touch and we'll make it work.

Is production concierge included as part of a full session booking?

+

Core production coordination is included with every MUSA session booking. Additional services such as accommodation, airport transfers, and extended music preparation can be added based on your project's needs. We'll outline everything in your quote.

Can remote clients receive the same level of production support?

+

Absolutely. Remote clients receive the same coordination — score preparation, technical setup, orchestra management, and real-time communication throughout the session. The only difference is that our team acts as your on-site proxy, keeping you informed and in control at every stage.

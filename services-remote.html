How It Works

Three Steps to Your Session

1

Upload Your Score

You upload your score and reference tracks. We handle the music preparation and orchestra contracting on our end.

2

We Record — You Direct

We record with you monitoring live via Source-Connect or ListenTo. Real-time talkback, optional video feed — you direct every take.

3

Receive Your Stems

You receive stems within 24 hours of session end, labeled, in Pro Tools format and ready for your post-production pipeline.

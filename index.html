Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you record?

+

We work with Prague's finest scoring stages including Smecky Music Studios, CNSO Studios, Rudolfinum / Dvorak Hall, and Czech Television Music Studios. We recommend the best room for your project's size and acoustic needs.

Can I direct the session remotely?

+

Absolutely. Our remote scoring setup includes Source-Connect Pro, ListenTo, secure 4K multi-camera video, and real-time talkback. Composers direct sessions daily from LA, London, Tokyo, and beyond.

What's included in a typical project?

+

MUSA is full-service: orchestra contracting, conductor, engineers, studio booking, music preparation, session recording, mixing, and stem delivery. We also handle accommodation, transport, and hospitality if you visit Prague.

How far in advance do I need to book?

+

Typical lead time is 2-6 weeks, though we've accommodated rush projects in under two weeks. The earlier you reach out, the more flexibility we have with scheduling and venue selection.

How do you handle IP and buyout?

+

All MUSA recordings are 100% buyout. No residuals, no union complexity, no ongoing fees. You receive total intellectual property ownership in perpetuity. Simple contracts, complete clarity.
